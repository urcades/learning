I currently lead Tlon’s design efforts on a variety of fronts, from co-architecting its flagship product lines alongside execs and PMs, to working alongside engineers to refine nitty-gritty elements of various web and native UIs, to collaborating with my favorite designers in the world on soulful marketing-communications materials, to pitching the future of our work to investors. As a classic startup designer, I’ve worn a variety of hats and touched nearly everything we’ve ever produced. I’ve hired most of the design team and some of the marketing and communications team(s). I’ve led the charge in getting Tlon to adopt its own products for work communications, and I’ve essentially set the aesthetic bar for the Urbit protocol/network and see my influence reflected in the work of dozens of other organizations on the network. It’s hard to summarize everything I’ve accomplished at Tlon, but in a nutshell: I’ve designed a new networking protocol, operating system, app suite, software ecosystem, and corporate design approach from 0 to 1, and I’ve contributed directly to 10x-ing the size of the Urbit network from 2022 to 2023.
